00:01 HOW IT WORKS

From link to answer in seconds

STEP 01

Paste a link

Any YouTube URL — lectures, podcasts, tutorials, shorts — in any caption language. VidSage pulls the full transcript.

STEP 02

We index every second

The transcript is chunked with timestamps and embedded into a multilingual vector store on your machine.

STEP 03

Ask anything, in any language

Answers stream in live, in the language you asked in, with clickable timestamps that jump the player to the source moment.
